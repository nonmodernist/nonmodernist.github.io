The idea is simple: I want to build a wiki that collects the various stuff I use to teach different classes. Things like: the timeline of optical toys that I give my multimedia authoring students, or the list of folk horror tropes that I developed after teaching a genre in film course on the topic.

There are any number of solutions to this problem, but I can't seem to sort out which works best. They all have some kind of friction that prevents me from feeling like, "ah yes, this is right, this works."

Maybe the trouble is that I want to skip ahead to the part where I have pages and pages of material, enough that it feels hefty and substantial. I long for my own filmsite.org. And I'm certainly not there yet.
