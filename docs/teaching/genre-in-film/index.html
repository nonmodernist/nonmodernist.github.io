    Genre in Film (ENGL 20753) #

    Genre in Film is an intermediate-level film course I created for the English Department at TCU. I taught the first instance of this course in Fall 2024, with a folk horror theme. In the future, I hope to teach the course on film noir.

    ðŸ“‚ Catalog Description: Studies of the aesthetic, ideological, and historical dimensions of film genre(s) such as film noir, horror, romantic comedy, the musical, etc, with examples drawn from global film industries. Topics will vary by semester and may include a comparative approach to genre.

    The course is designed to work alongside other film offerings in the English Department, including the introductory-level Approaches to Film, upper-level Theories of Cinema and Literature & Film, and the practicum-style Multimedia Authoring: Animation & Film. I also plan to create an additional intermediate-level course, Movements in Film, thus significantly expanding the number of film courses students can take in the English Department.
